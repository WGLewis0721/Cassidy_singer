About Cass

Singer • Entertainer • Performer

Cassidy Accasia — known on stage simply as Cass — is a powerhouse vocalist and entertainer based in Alabama with a presence that commands any room. Blending soulful delivery with high-energy performance, Cass creates unforgettable experiences for audiences of every size — from intimate private gatherings to sold-out stages.

Whether you're looking for the perfect centerpiece for your wedding, a headline act for a corporate event, or a captivating live show at your venue, Cass delivers professionalism and passion in every performance. Let's work.
